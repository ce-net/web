Live log

idle

A read-only viewer joined to /rt/<app>/__debug. Apps publish JSON frames there — {"t":"log","level":"info","msg":"…"}, {"t":"trace","name":"render","ms":12}, {"t":"metric","name":"fps","value":60}. The hub replays recent history on join, then streams live. You only see frames from the app's own clients (this viewer publishes nothing).

platform /stats

Caps stop anonymous writers from filling the relay disk. Signed/owned writes earn larger, identity-scoped quotas (see docs/limits). These are the platform globals, not your per-app quota.
